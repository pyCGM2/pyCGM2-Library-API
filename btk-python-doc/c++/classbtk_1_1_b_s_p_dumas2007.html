Detailed Description

Add BSPs to a given model using the data published by Dumas et al. (2007).

The corrigendum for the BSP of the head and neck were taken into account.

Reference
Dumas R., Chèze L., Verriest J.-P.
Adjustments to McConville et al. and Young et al. body segment inertial parameters
Journal of Biomechanics, 2007, 40(3), 543-553.

Dumas R., Chèze L., Verriest J.-P.
Corrigendum to "Adjustments to McConville et al. and Young et al. body segment inertial parameters"
Journal of Biomechanics, 2007, 40(7), 1651-1652.
