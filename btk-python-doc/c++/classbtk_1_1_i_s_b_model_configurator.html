void btk::ISBModelConfigurator::Configure ( Model *  model,
BodyPart  b,
Side  s 
) const
virtual

Configure the model by defining these segments and joints. The ISB configuration is defined with:

  • 3 segments by upper limb (arm, foream and hand);
  • 3 segments by lower limb (thigh, shank and foot);
  • 1 torso segment;
  • 1 pelvis segment;
  • 3 relative joints by upper limb (shoulder, elbow and wrist);
  • 3 relative joints by lower limb (hip, knee and ankle);
  • 1 relative joint betweem the torso and the pelvis;
  • 1 global joint for the torso (thorax);
  • 4 fixed chains:
    • Left/Right lower limbs: thigh, shank and foot
    • Left/Right upper limbs: arm, foream and hand
