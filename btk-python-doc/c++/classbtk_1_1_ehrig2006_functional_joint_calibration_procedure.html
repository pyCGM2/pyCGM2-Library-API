Detailed Description

Functional calibration procedure based on Ehrig et al. (2006).

This calibration procedure use the motion of two consecutive segments to find the center of the ball joint between them. The segments used are selected by the joint's label given to the static method New.

Reference
Ehrig R.M.; Taylor W.R.; Duda G.N.; Heller M.O.
A survey of formal methods for determining the centre of rotation of ball joints
Journal of Biomechanics, 2006, 39(15), 2798-2809.
