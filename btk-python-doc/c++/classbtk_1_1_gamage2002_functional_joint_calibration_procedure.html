Detailed Description

Functional calibration procedure based on Gamage and Lasenby (2002).

This calibration procedure use the motion of the proximal segment and the markers attached on the distal segment. The proximal segment used is selected by the joint's label given to the static methods New. The markers used are selected by the given labels to the static methods New.

Reference
Gamage S.S.H.U.; Lasenby J.
New least squares solutions for estimating the average centre of rotation and the axis of rotation
Journal of Biomechanics, 2002, 35(1), 87-93.
